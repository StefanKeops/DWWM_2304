{{LangMenu}}{{-}} {{Italy Menu}} {{TOCright}} In 2012, Congress elections were held on the 25th of the month and Congress was inaugurated one day later - on the 26th. Until September citizens voted for an individual party [[Congress candidate]] in a certain region and from September citizens voted for the national party list instead of individual candidates. This is the complete overview of the 2012 Italian [[Congress elections]].
