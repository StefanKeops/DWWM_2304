{{Italy Menu}}{{-}} {{TOCright}} ==January== Results unavailable ==February== Results unavailable ==March== The '''Congress of Italy for March 2014''' was elected on March 26th 2014. 478 citizens voted in this election.
Congress served from March 26th 2014 to April 26th 2014 and it was composed of 39 [[Congress member|Congress members]] 40th seat is missing because the Party President of Partito Comunista eItaliano forgot to rearrange the list order and to ask party members to candidate in order to properly fill the list. Something similar already occurred in [[Congress of Italy 2012#November|November 2012]].. The '''[[Speaker|Chairman of Congress]]''' of this month was [[PDCI98]] (3rd Term).
